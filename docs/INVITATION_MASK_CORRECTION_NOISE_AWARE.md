## Interpretation

The feedback-noise sweep should be read as a reliability boundary. Mask correction reduces missed opportunities by trusting the aggregate current feedback count, but larger count noise can turn that trust into extra failed invitations. The clipped variants test whether limiting per-slot target-count changes can recover high-noise robustness without changing the IRS candidate-generation stage.
